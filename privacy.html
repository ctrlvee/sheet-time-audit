Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 12, 2025

1. Overview

Sheet Time & Audit is a Google Workspace Add-on that helps users track active editing time in Google Sheets and review project-level audit data. This policy describes how user information is handled.

2. Data Collection and Storage

  • The Add-on does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information outside your Google Account.
  • All usage data (such as time tracking and sheet activity) is stored locally using Google’s UserProperties and ScriptProperties services.
  • No data is sent to external servers or shared with the developer.

3. Permissions and Access

The Add-on may request access to:

  • View and manage your spreadsheets (to log activity and retrieve version history).
  • Access Google Drive file metadata (to display file owner and revision data).
These permissions are used solely for the stated functionality and are required for the Add-on to operate correctly.

4. User Control

You can uninstall the Add-on at any time from the Google Workspace Marketplace. Uninstalling will remove the Add-on and any locally stored properties within your Google account.

5. Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, please contact the developer via the Support Page or open an issue on GitHub.
