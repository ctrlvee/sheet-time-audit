Sheet Time & Audit

A Google Workspace Add-on for Google Sheets

Sheet Time & Audit helps users monitor their spreadsheet productivity and review project-level collaboration data. It automatically tracks active editing time and provides a one-click audit of contributors and version history.

This add-on is designed for educational, research, and portfolio demonstration purposes. It does not transmit user data externally and operates entirely within the Google Apps Script environment.
